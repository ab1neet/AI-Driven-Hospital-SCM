About the Project

This AI-driven hospital supply chain risk management system was developed to address the critical challenges healthcare facilities face in managing their supply chains, especially during emergencies.

Using advanced machine learning algorithms and optimization techniques, the system provides real-time insights, accurate forecasts, and actionable recommendations to ensure the continuous availability of critical medical supplies.
